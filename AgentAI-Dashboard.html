  • The dollar amount associated to the above unbilled service lines
  • The average delay (in days) between the service date and the invoice date when billing is complete *** We should show this metric for Last 14 Days, Last 30 days, Last 90 days to identify trend improvement/decline

Unified Payment Posting & Reconciliation Dashboard

Dashboard Purpose:
This report is designed to provide a centralized view of payment posting activity and accuracy across all 24 clinics. It enables Clinical Billing Solutions and AgentAI teams to monitor:
  • The volume and timeliness of payments being posted from all sources (EDI 835s, paper EOBs, converted files)
  • The source breakdown of payment postings (e.g., Stedi, Trizetto, manual)
  • The accuracy of reconciliation between charges, payments, and adjustments
  • The identification of discrepancies, including unmatched or unapplied payments, and traceability to specific payment IDs and sources
